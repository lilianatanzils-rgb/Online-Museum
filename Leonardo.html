Leonardo da Vinci

Artist • Inventor • Visionary

About Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo da Vinci (1452–1519) was an Italian Renaissance master known for his unmatched talent in art, science, engineering, and anatomy. His curiosity and imagination made him one of the greatest minds in history.

Leonardo produced masterpieces that shaped the future of art, combining realism, emotion, and groundbreaking techniques.

Famous Works

Mona Lisa (1503)

Famous for her smile; one of the most recognized paintings in the world.

The Last Supper (1498)

A groundbreaking mural showing the moment Jesus reveals that one of them will betray him.

Vitruvian Man (1490)

A symbol of the blend between science and art during the Renaissance.
